About Daniel’s Wildlife & Pest

I don’t just set traps and move on — I build strategic, customized plans to fully remove wildlife and pests from your property and make sure they stay out. With over 8 years of experience throughout the Salt Lake Valley, I’ve personally handled thousands of jobs, and I bring that same level of care, precision, and problem-solving to every single one.

Over the years, I’ve helped build large pest and wildlife companies from the ground up — giving them the systems, structure, and reputation they needed to grow. But as they expanded, I saw their quality slip. Services became rushed, problems were patched instead of solved, and customers were left with the bare minimum. That’s why I started Daniel’s Wildlife & Pest — so I could deliver the kind of work I believe people deserve.

When you work with me, you’re getting that same high-level expertise and professionalism — every time. I’m the owner and the technician, so I personally handle every job from start to finish. No shortcuts. No guesswork. Just clean, effective results.

I offer a full range of pest and wildlife services, including:

  • Wildlife removal – raccoons, skunks, squirrels, bats, birds, and more
  • Rodent & ground pest control – rats, mice, voles, gophers
  • Insect control – ants, spiders, wasps, bed bugs, cockroaches, and general pests
  • Termite inspections & treatments
  • Entry prevention & exclusion – sealing up access points so they stay gone
  • Cleanup & disinfection – removal of waste, odors, and harmful pathogens
  • Damage repair – fixing wildlife entry and contamination
  • IPM-based solutions – smart, long-term pest control strategies

I love what I do, and it shows in the results. Whether it’s a small insect issue or a major attic infestation, I bring the same energy, detail, and care to every job.
